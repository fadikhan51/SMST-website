SMST, the School of Musical Science and Technology, is an exciting project led by Sudhanshu (aka Ony) focused on advancing Indian electronic music production.
It began with a small group of passionate friends and community members who wanted to create a space where music technology and production could be explored in depth.
At SMST, we're dedicated to fostering a vibrant learning environment where students and mentors can grow together. We believe in empowering our students to explore their musical potential and push the boundaries of what they can achieve.

Shudhansu Kumar
In the field of music production, Sudhanshu, also known as Mini Onion or Ony, is an enthusiastic mentor and teacher. He is a total Ableton nerd who loves audio programming and sound design because they combine technical skill with artistic expression.

When Sudhanshu established a deep connection with the technical components of music, that's when his adventure started. Motivated by musicians such as LeKtriQue, Haterade, and Erosin Music, he immersed himself in the realm of music production, both technique and science. His proficiency with Ableton Live enables him to create intricate, dynamic compositions out of straightforward tunes.

As a mentor, Sudhanshu is dedicated to imparting to young producers and musicians his expertise and love of sound exploration. His method of instruction empowers students by fusing technical expertise with creative inquiry.
